Plante Details

Plant Name: Aloe Vera

Region: Africa, Middle East

Properties:

  • Moisturizing
  • Anti-inflammatory
  • Antioxidant

Precautions:

  • Avoid excessive use on sensitive skin
  • Do not ingest large amounts

Drug Interactions:

  • May interact with diuretics
  • Can affect blood sugar levels

Description:

Aloe vera is a succulent plant species of the genus Aloe. It is widely used for its medicinal and cosmetic properties, especially for skin care and hydration.
